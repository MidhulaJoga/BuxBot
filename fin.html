Financial Tips

  • Track your income and expenses diligently to understand where every rupee goes.
  • Save at least 3-6 months' worth of living expenses in an easily accessible, liquid account.
  • Focus on paying off high-interest debts (like credit cards) first to save on interest costs.
  • Even small, consistent investments can grow significantly over time due to the power of compounding.
  • Set up automatic transfers from your checking to savings/investment accounts each payday.
  • Revisit your budget and goals at least once a year.
  • Spread investments across different asset classes.
  • Learn about tax-saving instruments and deductions to maximize your take-home pay.
  • Continuously learn about personal finance, economic trends, and investment opportunities.
